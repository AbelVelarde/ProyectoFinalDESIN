Email Actions



Here you can see how to manage an email.

For creating a new email you need to open the menu "Correos" and go
to the submenu "Nuevo Correo". This will display a new window.




In order we have the field for your account (you have to choose one of your registered accounts)
and also the fields for the account your are sending the email and the subject.
Finally your can see the editor for entering the email body.


If you want to delete an existing email, you need to go to the submenu or the
button in the menu above the email. Those options are only available if you have an email selected
such as the answer and resend buttons.





For answer and resending an email you can use the proper buttons. With this options, some fields are
preloaded and a predefined text will be shown on the message editor.
